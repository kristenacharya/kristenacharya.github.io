Hi there! My name is Kristen Acharya, and I'm a software engineer. Five years ago, after hastily completing a Udemy Web Development course, I was offered an internship at a digital agency. Shortly after, I was hired on full time and allowed to work on a broad spectrum of projects.

I spent the next three years tackling legacy projects written in ColdFusion, PHP, and early versions of Laravel. I also contributed my own code to some new projects in Laravel, Phalcon, and Vue. A good portion of my time was spent writing MySQL queries to produce custom data sets for clients, and this remains my favorite task.

My current role allows me the best of all worlds: abundant query writing and data analysis, coding in new (to me) frameworks and languages, and producing/interacting with API's to share data between all the platforms we use.

The rest of my time is devoted to my little family. My husband of 15 years is a web solutions architect, and we have a 3 year old daughter.
